Argolia Events

Argolia is a world (a Minecraft Server) where friends can join together to mine, build and grow their Kingdom. We’ll be hosting this so that everyone at the holiday program can be on the same world! The in-game economy means resources can be traded and empires grown. Developing skills means more abilities are unlocked. Crafting magical items is possible and watch out for the vampires! When the day ends, the world of Argolia and your Kingdom will continue. You will be able to join your friends online and continue and further expand your empire.
